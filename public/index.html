About The Page

This is a Multi Unit Converter site. You input a number and select what unit you want to convert it to.
There are four units that you can convert to; Length, Temperature, Weight, Pressure.
Under Length, there are 8 units which are Meter, Centi-Meter, Mili-Meter, Kilo-Meter, Mile, Foot, Yard, Inch.
Under Temperature, there are 4 units which are Celcius, Kelvin, Rankine, Fahrenheit.
Under Weight, there are 6 units which are Gram, Kilogram, Ounce, Pound, Carat, Metric-Ton
and lastly under Pressure, there are 4 units which are Pascal, Torr, Barr, Standard Atmospheric Pressure.
Enjoy your time here and watch out for more of my projects.
